How to play?

1)roll the two dice.

2)Add the numbers on the dice together to get your roll total.

3)Use your roll total to flip down one or more tiles that correspond to the numbers rolled.

4)For example, if you roll a 5 and a 3, you can choose to flip down the tile numbered 8 (5 + 3) or the tiles numbered 5 and 3 individually.

5)If you manage to flip down all the tiles, you "shut the box" and win the round.

6)the player's score is the sum of the numbers left standing on the tiles.

7)The goal is to have the lowest score or flip down all the tiles.
